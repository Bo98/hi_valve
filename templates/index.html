TF2 Blog Discord Webhook

Posts a message in your very own Discord server when TF2.com releases a new entry on their RSS feed (could be Blog, News or Updates).

To find your webhook URL, go on Discord, Server Settings, Webhooks, Create one and paste the URL below.

If you don't want to use it anymore, just delete the webhook from your server.
